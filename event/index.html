Financing and developing agriculture through collaboration

The agricultural sector has long been neglected in Nigeria. This
event has been created to provide an inclusive community for the agro-ecosystem by presenting opportunities in the agricultural sector to both farmers and investors.

To address issues faced by farmers in the 21st century. Ultimately, improving the agricultural value chain in Nigeria.

Research

One of the aims of this event is to bring relevant applied research to the fore through presentations from researchers.

  • Agricultural trade commerce
  • Food security

  • Agriculture trade systems
  • Farming mechanization
  • Modern farm practices
  • Agric value chain
